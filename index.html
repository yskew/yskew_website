The Secret Blueprint

If you go first: Take a corner. Next, claim another corner to create a "fork" (two ways to win at once), forcing a guaranteed victory.

If you go second: You are playing for a draw. If they take a corner, you must take the center immediately to block their trap.

Bottom Line: First player attacks the corners; second player survives by holding the center.
